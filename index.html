Time Series Analysis of MOMA Acquisitions Grouped by Departments.

Presented by Kelechi Vincent Iwuagwu

This Visual centers on the temporal analysis of the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) dataset, examining the evolution of its collection over the last 150 years.

Data Sources:

This visual utilize MoMA's dataset, comprising 140,848 records of artworks and 15,243 artist records, providing a comprehensive view of the museum's evolving collection.

The analysis will involve creating visualizations to showcase the Artworks acquisition by departments (seven categories of artworks, which are Drawings & Prints, Architecture & Design, Painting & Sculpture, Photography, Fluxus Collection, Media & Performance, and Film) over the years
